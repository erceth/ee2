About Eric Ethington

I graduated in Computer Science from Brigham Young University in April 2013. I have been working as a Front-end Web developer ever since. While in college I had an internship for a year where I got my taste for web development and loved it! That's when I decided I would web development would be my career.

My focus is building Javascript heavy websites. I have a lot of experience with AngularJS. I have also used Knockout, Angular 2 and plain ol' Javascript. In conjunction with Javascript I use Node.js, LESS, Bootstrap, Gulp, Bower, jQuery, Socket.io, Grunt, Git, Mercurial, Gimp 2, Wordpress, SQL database, Yeoman and PHP.

Learning new technologies is not a problem for me. A great app can be built in just about anything. The current hot technology takes a back seat to a healthy development process with good communication, testing and code reviews.

I refrain from becoming emotionally attached to my code. They are just dumb bits that need improvement. Typically while working I will make my first attempt at something and share it with others. Together we will discuss if it is perfect or needs improvement. A team player is someone that puts code quality above personal recognition.

Some of the my other interests include learning French, working out, gardening, learning guitar and studying Psychology.

The purpose of ericethington.com is to show off my coding projects.

Please enjoy browsing through my website and my projects.
